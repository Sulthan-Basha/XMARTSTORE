About Us

XM Art Store, established in 2016 was created with the sole purpose of helping customers in the Home Décor and Giftware industries. The objective is to provide our customers with full-service capabilities: designing exclusive products, manufacturing them following stringent QC guidelines and arranging transportation that works for you. We make your supply chain logistics smooth and transparent. We have the ability to take your projects from the conceptualization stage to shipping you the finished goods, while keeping you always informed of the progress. You can take possession of the goods either in Asia (China/ India), or we can ship them to the port of your convenience in North America (Point of Entry - POE). We handle your supply chain, so you have time to manage things that are important to you.

We have 8 designers on staff in the USA and 2 designers in Asia. We can turn your mood board/concept to finished design quickly and professionally. We can arrange samples, production scheduled, quality control visits, container consolidation, and can also negotiate ocean freight on your behalf. We communicate each project’s mile stones, and stay on schedule until the product is delivered to your door.

Our goal is to always EXCEED YOUR EXPECTATIONS.
